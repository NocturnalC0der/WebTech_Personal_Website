About Me

I was born in Wales, but lived my whole life so far in western Slovakia.

I have two younger brothers. Growing up, I was part of a small home-schooling group, founded by a group of mothers, who wanted a different kind of primary education for their kids.

Keeping a healthy, strong and good-looking body is always my top priority.

Learning from great minds of the past and present, and searching for inspiring ideas to expand my knowledge and elevate my consciousness.

Going hand-in-hand with physical training, healthy nutrition is both beneficial and enjoyable

Progressing towards financial independence by working on meaningful projects combines the necessary and the enjoyable in pursuit of the desirable.

Strength Training

Fitness has been a passion of mine for 7 years. Building a healthy, strong and aesthetic body is a noble pursuit in my eyes, and everyone should be involved in it to some degree. I started with just bodyweight exercises, eventually incorporated external weights as well, making me a well-rounded strength athlete.

Reading

The first books I remember really reading were the Harry Potter series. Our grandparents had the Slovak version, and at home, we had the English versions. From age 10 to age 16, I read the whole trilogy a total of 5 times, twice in Slovak, and three times in English. I think I will reread it in the future someday. Today, I like ready factual, self-help books, that expose me to great ideas mainly in the realms of business and health.

Nutrition

I take my food quite seriously. When I started my fitness journey in 2018, I made a rule to myself that I'm not going to eat processed food, or sweets. That was the first rule that I followed in terms of nutrition. With time, along with my brother, who is now a lot better at cooking than me, I learned to prepare basic meals for myself, and became more interested in the topic of healthy nutrition, and how our food choices also relate to our environment.
